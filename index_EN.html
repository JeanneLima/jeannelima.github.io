A little About Me

I am a Web Design Medalist at the Olympiad of Knowledge at the Senai School and State Stages. I am also graduated in the Multimedia course, having studied at C.E. José Leite Lopes / NAVE. And, currently, I study Graphic Design at Veiga de Almeida University.

I am familiar with different areas of multimedia performance and agile methodologies of project management through projects in multidisciplinary teams..I love photography, music, 2D and 3D animation, books, movies and series. I am also interested in acessibility, sports, education and technology. Besides, I love learning and I can easily do it.

Kakurenbo Game

Kakurenbo is a multiplayer first person computer game based on Japanese urban legends. In it, children play hide-and-seek (kakurenbo) with evil spirits present there. Children should look for a way to escape the city, while the spirits materialized in monsters must hunt them.

Did you enjoy the project? So be sure to check out the complete project on Behance.net and on the game page on Facebook.
